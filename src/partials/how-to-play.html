How To Play?

Here's how it works

  • Develop Your Kingdom

    Construct buildings
    and grow your economy.

  • Train Armies

    Recruit and strengthen your military forces.

  • Expand Territory

    Capture new areas
    to increase influence.

  • Strengthen Alliances

    Cooperate with others
    to gain advantage.

  • Engage in Battles

    Fight other players
    in strategic combat.
